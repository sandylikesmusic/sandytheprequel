SANDY

This is the story of an endearing character on the autistic spectrum.
Only one thing resonates with him, and that one thing is music. 

Watch now Learn more



Sandy is an Autistic Savant, meaning he has a natural skill and obsession with music. He simply lives and breathes music, understands it and lets it dictate his life. His instrument of choice is the piano. Music flows through him, but expressing this and being taken seriously is something that weighs heavily on his shoulders.

‘Cheek to cheek’

One of Sandy's favourite tune! By Louis Armstrong and Ella Fitzgerald.
"He sways to the music, smiling to himself and subtly mouthing the lyrics out, loving every
second of it."

Sandy's job

Sandy works at Kelly's, a records store in Cardiff city center, He loves playing records and singing and dancing to them as he workds. That's what makes him happy.

Sandy's concern

He's so attached to the records he plays in the store that he hates
selling those to the store's customers.
